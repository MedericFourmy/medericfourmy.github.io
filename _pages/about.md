I am a robotics researcher at [CIIRC](https://www.ciirc.cvut.cz/) in the team of [Josef Sivic](https://people.ciirc.cvut.cz/~sivic/). My research revolves around perception for robotics, like object pose estimation, model predictive control, and robot state estimation. I am part of the [BOP](https://bop.felk.cvut.cz/home/) challenge team, where I manage the evaluation framework and actively contribute to the open-source [toolkit](https://github.com/thodan/bop_toolkit). Before joining CIIRC in 2022, I obtained a PhD at [Laas](https://www.laas.fr/) under the supervision of [Nicolas Mansard](https://gepettoweb.laas.fr/index.php/Members/NicolasMansard) and [Joan Solà](http://www.joansola.eu/) on Factor Graph state estimation for legged-robots. I hold a Master's degree in Aerospace engineering and Data science from [Supaero](https://www.isae-supaero.fr/), which was completed by a Master's Thesis at [Heriott-Watt University](https://www.hw.ac.uk/) on [underwater robotics](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZkgKq-Zxk3w).
